5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to ensure the Service functions correctly and securely.

Cookie Table
	•	session – Essential. Maintains your logged-in session. Duration: session (deleted when browser closes). Set by: KiCoMa.
	•	csrf_token – Essential. Protects against cross-site request forgery. Duration: session. Set by: KiCoMa.
	•	remember_token – Functional. Keeps you logged in between sessions (if selected). Duration: session. Set by: KiCoMa.
	•	language_preference – Functional. Stores your language selection. Duration: session. Set by: KiCoMa.
	•	_ga, _ga_* – Analytics. Google Analytics 4 usage statistics. Duration: up to 2 years. Set by: Google LLC.

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) with IP anonymization enabled.
You can disable analytics cookies through your browser settings or by using Google’s Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

We do not use advertising or marketing cookies.
